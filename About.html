About

I am from Sparta, New Jersey and am a Kean University senior majoring in Information Technology focusing on the cybersecurity option. I am to graduate in the Spring of 2023 with a Bachelors in the above major. I am in a relationship which I've now been in for over a year. My partner is also at college at Montclair State University, studying Early Childhood Development focusing on the Childhood disabilities option. She is aiming to get her Masters in the previously mentioned degree. In my free time, I enjoy reading science fiction, primarily from the late Michael Crichton; writing science fiction; and watching tokusatsu, a Japanese television genre focusing on suited actors fighting an evil they are somehow related to.

I enjoy reading science fiction, primarily from the late Michael Crichton. I have currently finished reading Jurassic Park and The Lost World: Jurassic Park. I am in the process of reading Prey, a book focusing on nanotechnology.

As previously stated, I enjoy writing science fiction in my freetime. My writing involves the multiverse and its unlimited possibilities. My writing has stories involving cyborgs to almost mythical beings born of the cosmos. Some stories involve the creation of monsters such as the Terrors or the Inhumanoids. All of the stories intertwine though resulting in characters getting to know their counterparts. The big bad of the first few phases is an elder god of the multiverse born at its creation, whom became fed up with his brothers and sisters, and destroyed them all.

As mentioned above, I enjoy watching tokusatsu, a Japanese television genre. The tokusatsu genre is a mix of drama, science fiction, and superhero. Tokusatsu translates to "specail filmmaking" as the suit acting is where the specail filming comes from. I got into the genre back in 2017 during my first year of community college due to what the next season of Power Rangers was to be based on which was Tokumei Sentai Go-Busters, which celebrated the American adaption of the Japanese genre. From there, I went on to watch several movies and television shows of the genre with my favorite primary Kamen Riders being: Zero-One, Zi-O, Build, Gaim, OOO, Den-O, and Kuuga. My favorite secondary Kamen Riders are: Geiz, Cross-Z, Baron, and Zeronos. My favorite tertiary Kamen Riders are: Woz, Grease, Chaser, and Ryugen. My favorite quaternary Kamen Riders are: Demons, Rogue, Snipe, and Zangestsu (Shin). My favorite Dark Kamen Riders are: Ark-Zero, Blood, Evolt, and Poseidon.
